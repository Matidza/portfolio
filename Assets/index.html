Admission

Still In Development


Admission is an innovative online platform that revolutionizes the school application process for parents. With Admission, parents can effortlessly apply for their children's school enrollment from the comfort of their homes, eliminating the need to visit schools or handle paper applications. The app offers a streamlined experience, providing all necessary information and documents in one convenient location. By prioritizing accessibility and efficiency, Admission simplifies the journey to securing a bright future for students.


Backend:
  • Python
  • Django
  • PostgreSQL
Frontend:
  • HTML
  • CSS
  • Bootstrap

The website features an intuitive design that includes dedicated portals for both parents and schools. Parents have access to a personalized dashboard where they can manage their applications, track status updates, and receive real-time notifications about important deadlines and requirements. Meanwhile, schools benefit from a separate portal that allows them to efficiently track applications, review submissions, and communicate with families. This dual approach enhances collaboration and transparency, ensuring a smooth admission process for everyone involved.
